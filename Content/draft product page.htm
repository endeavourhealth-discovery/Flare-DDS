Discovery data distribution service

Posts patient data from multiple sources, in a common format, and under strict governance, to anyone who has a right and a reason to see the data. Data is exchanged via either secure and authenticated file transfer or secure and authenticated Care Connect FHIR APIs.



Discovery record access service

Enables third party systems to access structured patient data from multiple sources. Data is exchanged via secure and authenticated Care Connect FHIR APIs.

Enables third party systems to access structured patient data from multiple sources. Data is exchanged via secure and authenticated Care Connect FHIR APIs.
