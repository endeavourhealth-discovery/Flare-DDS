Discovery record access service

Enables third party systems to access structured patient data from multiple sources.



Decision Support

Decision Support applies rules, formulae, and algorithms to patient data to highlight patient risk or issues to support clinical decisions for direct patient care.



Discovery data distribution service

Posts patient data from multiple sources, in a common format, and under strict governance, to anyone who has a right and a reason to see the data. Data is exchanged via either secure and authenticated file transfer or secure and authenticated Care Connect FHIR APIs.



Population Reporting

Population Reporting provides the results of advanced queries and data analysis, and distributes those results as data files.



Data Sharing Manager

Use Data Sharing Manager to configure data sharing agreements and data processing agreements.



Database Support

Supporting third parties to host their own patient data.
