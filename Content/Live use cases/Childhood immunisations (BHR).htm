Childhood immunisations (BHR)

The childhood immunisations extract provides Barking and Dagenham, Havering & Redbridge (BHR) CCGs with child heath data from GP systems.

The DDS delivers an extract that contains changes in GP immunisation data as specified in the required datasets.

The extract includes data from all patients who are aged between 0-19 years old and who are registered at BHR CCG GP practices.

Project population

All residents with a Tower Hamlets address.

Project cohort

All patients.
